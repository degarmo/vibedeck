Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

VibeDeck is designed to collect no personal data. This privacy policy describes how the app handles information.

What we collect

Nothing. VibeDeck does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. We have no servers, no analytics, no third-party SDKs, and no telemetry.

Where your data lives

All prompts, folders, and settings you create in VibeDeck are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud Drive enabled, your data is also synced through Apple's CloudKit private database — meaning it lives in your own iCloud account and never passes through any system we operate.

You can review and delete this iCloud data at any time via Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage on your Apple device.

What we do not do

VibeDeck does not collect prompts, names, email addresses, IP addresses, location, or any other personal information. The app does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies. There are no third parties to share data with because there is no data to share.

Children's privacy

VibeDeck is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If the way VibeDeck handles data ever changes, this policy will be updated and the date at the top revised.
