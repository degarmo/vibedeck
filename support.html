How do I sync between Mac and iPhone? +

Sign into the same iCloud account on both devices, install VibeDeck on both, and your prompts sync automatically within seconds. iCloud Drive must be enabled.

Where is my data stored? +

Locally on your device, with optional sync through your own iCloud private database. Nothing routes through any server we operate.

Can I export my prompts? +

Yes. Settings → Export prompts gives you a JSON file you can save, share, or import into another VibeDeck install.

Do you collect any data about me? +

No. VibeDeck has no analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs.

How do I report a bug? +

Email degarmo@gmail.com with a short description and a screenshot if relevant.
