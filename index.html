Features

Built for the way you actually prompt.

Four ideas, no clutter. VibeDeck stays out of the way until the moment you need a prompt — then puts it on your clipboard.

01

Tap to copy

Cards on a 4×2 grid. Tap one, prompt is on your clipboard.

02

Variables

Templates with {name} or {project} open a fill-in sheet before copying.

03

iCloud sync

Your prompts sync between Mac and iPhone via your own iCloud. No servers.

04

Folders & pinning

Color-coded folders, pinned prompts surface to the top.
